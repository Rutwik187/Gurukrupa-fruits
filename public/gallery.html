Product Life Cycle

01

work directly with local farmers:

We work directly with local farmers who share our commitment to quality. By building strong relationships with our growers, we can ensure that we're getting the best mangos possible.

02

We hand-pick our mangos:

We hand-pick our mangos to ensure that only the best make it onto our website. Our team of expert pickers knows how to select the juiciest, most flavorful mangos, and they take great care to handle them gently to prevent bruising.

03

Quality Control:

We have a strict quality control process in place to ensure that only the best mangos make it onto our website. Each fruit is inspected for quality and ripeness, and any that don't meet our standards are discarded.

04

Cold Storage:

We store our mangos in temperature-controlled environments to preserve their freshness and flavor. This ensures that our customers receive mangos that are as fresh as possible.

05

Quality Packing:

We take packaging seriously, too. Our mangos are carefully packed in boxes designed to protect them during shipping. We use environmentally-friendly packaging whenever possible, and our boxes are fully recyclable.

06

Timely Delivery:

We work with reliable shipping partners to ensure that our mangos are delivered to our customers as quickly and efficiently as possible. This helps to reduce transit time and ensure that our mangos arrive in the best possible condition.
